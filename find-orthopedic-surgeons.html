About Orthopedic Surgery

Orthopedic surgery is a specialty focused on the diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of disorders of the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, muscles, and nerves.

Services We Offer

  • Joint replacement surgery (hip, knee, shoulder)
  • Spine surgery and treatment
  • Sports injury treatment
  • Fracture repair and trauma care
  • Arthroscopic procedures
  • Pediatric orthopedic care
